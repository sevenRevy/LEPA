## Design Context

### Users
Casual Reddit users and moderators use this extension while scanning live Reddit post pages. They need a fast, readable second opinion on whether a post looks normal, spammy, ragebait-like, or suspicious without stopping their browsing flow.

### Brand Personality
Calm, clean, trustworthy. The interface should feel composed and legible rather than alarming, even when it surfaces risk signals.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark, restrained, utility-first UI with subtle emphasis and measured contrast. IBM Plex typography and muted panel surfaces support a clean moderation-tool feel. Avoid noisy dashboards, aggressive warning aesthetics, and crowded metric layouts.

### Design Principles
1. Keep primary signals easy to scan in under two seconds.
2. Use spacing and grouping before adding extra containers or decoration.
3. Let warnings feel precise, not theatrical.
4. Default to low-friction reading for casual users while preserving enough structure for moderators.
5. Maintain a calm visual rhythm with tight local grouping and clearer separation between major sections.
